University Management System

Smart Student ERP for university management system is designed specifically for all kinds of universities. Smart Student ERP supports universities to build, manage, and extend their digital campus. It enables individuals, systems, and communities to interact seamlessly across campus in an environment which is efficient and gives adequate service delivery and personalized educational experiences.

With Smart Student ERP system you gain a community where students get the services they demand, faculty facilitate teaching, and departmental staff have the information and support to be more effective.

Institutions Management System

Smart Student ERP helps to monitor the entire group of institutions with a single login. The Chairman or the head of the groups of institutions can view the functioning of the groups of institutions and make systematic analysis and comparison within the Institutions. Smart Student ERP helps to avoid frequent visits to all institutions to know the progress and through that they can save the time and keep all institutions under one control. Smart Student ERP streamlines and integrate the operation processes and information flow in the institution to synchronize the resources namely Students, employee, material, money and books through information.

Smart Student ERP facilitates Institute-wide Integrated Information System Covering all functional areas like, Student Admission, promotion of the Student, Fee Collection, Generate Student Id card, Student Attendance & Leave, Employee Attendance & Leave, Maintain the Inventories, Suppliers and Publishers, Question Paper Creation, Employee Salary Creation, Daily Expenses, EOD, Maintain the Library Record for Student & Employee, Time Table Creations for Teachers and more.

Student can view their all academic details by logging into Smart Student ERP. We provide 130+ features to the College management system.
