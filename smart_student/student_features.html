STUDENT MANAGEMENT

Admission, Enrollment, Logins

This segment covers all the basic information about the students with auto generated Student UniqueID. Can perform settings to Fee, Transport, Hostel, Logins of student & also Import student data from Excel Sheet for enrollment.

Admission: student admission form that allows you to fill the details directly or import their detailsfrom excel sheet and admit students one by one.

Enrollment: Once the data is entered you can Enroll the students and generate UniqueID. Can promote students to their concerned classes. Using data filters you can find students quickly as per your search.

ACADEMICS

Consistent and continuous evaluation of students and their performances can be done using Smart Student. The progress of the student can be viewed on a regular basis both by the parents and teachers.

This evaluation of the students would help in overall improvisation of their performances in various subjects and also help teachers to provide additional inputs for the students for helping them with their performance through our School Management System.

  • All the student details can be maintained academic year wise with all the required details.
  • All the reports are available online and can be emailed as PDFs.
  • Customized Exam Management screens for Faculties.
  • Student History Management

ASSIGNMENT
